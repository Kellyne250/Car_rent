About Us

The word Volkswagen means "People's car" in German.
The company was founded in 1937 by the German Labour Front
to produce a car designed by Ferdinand Porsche. It is now a global brand
and the flagship brand of the Volkswagen Group, the largest automotive manufacturer
by worldwide sales in 2016 and 2017.
The company's biggest market is in China, which delivers 40 percent of its sales and profits.
